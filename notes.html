Risk levels:

Unknown: Players who are accused of cheating or confirmed of cheating with an unknown risk level.
Low: Players who are confirmed of cheating and only use cheats for entertainment purposes.
Medium: Players who play the game normally but use cheats to give them an advantage.
High: Players who cheat for the purpose of ruining the game for others.
Severe: Players who use cheats that bypass kicking or banning scripts, making them unstoppable.
